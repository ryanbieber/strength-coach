# Form Check

Use this section when the user uploads a video or asks for technique review.

## Read Order
1. [workflow.md](workflow.md)
2. [rubric.md](rubric.md)
3. [output-format.md](output-format.md)

## Scope
- Use video to assess technique, setup, rep execution, and obvious deviations from the target pattern.
- Compare the clip against known-good movement standards, not just against a single picture.
- Keep the feedback tied to the lift, the goal, and the athlete's tolerance.
