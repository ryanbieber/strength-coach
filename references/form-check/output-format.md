# Form Check Output Format

## Recommended Structure
- What looked good
- What looked off
- Most important fix
- Secondary fix if relevant
- Confidence level
- If a redo is needed because of a bad assumption or bad camera angle

## Language Rules
- Be direct and specific.
- Tie feedback to observable behavior.
- Avoid pretending certainty when the angle or quality is poor.
- If the clip cannot support a strong opinion, say that plainly.

## Example
```markdown
## Form Check

- Good: bracing and depth looked solid.
- Off: the bar drifts forward out of the bottom.
- Fix 1: keep the bar over midfoot and drive your chest up sooner.
- Fix 2: slow the eccentric slightly so the bottom position stays stacked.
- Confidence: medium. The side angle was usable, but the load may have changed your mechanics.
```
