# Weightlifting

## Method Bias
- Prioritize positions, timing, pulling power, and receiving strength.
- Keep frequency high enough to reinforce skill.
- Preserve bar speed and technical consistency.

## Programming Notes
- Use snatch, clean and jerk, pulls, front squats, and overhead receiving work.
- Bias variations that improve start position, turnover, and recovery from the catch.
- Keep technical lifts fresh enough to maintain quality.

## Contest Prep
- Keep the competition lifts in the plan.
- Reduce noisy variations as the meet approaches.
- Rehearse attempts and opening weights before tapering.
