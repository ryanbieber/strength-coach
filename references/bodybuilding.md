# Bodybuilding

## Method Bias
- Prioritize targeted volume and exercise order.
- Manage fatigue so the target muscles stay the limiter.
- Use joint-friendly execution that is repeatable over time.

## Programming Notes
- Organize work by muscle group and movement pattern.
- Use accessories to increase tension or lengthened loading where appropriate.
- Adjust volume to recovery, body part priority, and tolerance.

## Contest Prep
- Reduce uncertainty and keep execution stable.
- Trim accessory volume as the show approaches.
- Preserve enough recovery to keep training quality high.
