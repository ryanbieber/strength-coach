## Blend Rules
- Use the template to borrow structure, not exact exercise lists.
- Match the athlete's fatigue tolerance before matching the template's workload.
- Keep strongman event specificity and implement similarity ahead of generic barbell substitutions.
- For strongman, preserve the contest implement, event order, carry distances, loading order, and fatigue pattern as closely as possible.
- For weightlifting, preserve the snatch and clean and jerk, plus the positions, pulls, and receiving strength that support them.
- For bodybuilding, preserve the target muscle group, volume landmarks, and exercise order for the lagging areas.
- For peaking, shift from more volume to more specificity and more intensity.
