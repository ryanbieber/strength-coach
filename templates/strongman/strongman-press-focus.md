# Strongman Press Focus

## Gist
- Overhead-specific strongman block centered on log, axle, or other pressing implements.
- Built to improve the clean, rack position, and pressing strength under fatigue.

## Best Use
- Press-limited athletes.
- Show prep when overhead events matter most.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP.
- Best when the athlete already has a base and needs overhead-specific carryover.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: event press
- Day 2: upper back and triceps support
- Day 3: secondary press variation

## What To Steal
- Exact implement or closest simulation.
- Press-specific accessory work.
- Clean and rack practice when relevant.

## Blend Notes
- Reduce pressing novelty close to the show.
- Start with enough support work to build the press, then trim toward exact implement practice as the contest approaches.
