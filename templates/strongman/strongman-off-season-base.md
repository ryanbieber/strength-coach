# Strongman Off-Season Base

## Gist
- General strongman template for building work capacity before contest-specific prep.
- Focuses on hinges, overhead, carries, grip, and trunk strength without chasing max event fatigue.

## Best Use
- Off-season or early prep.
- Athletes who need more base work before contest specificity.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: overhead press + upper back
- Day 2: deadlift or hinge + trunk
- Day 3: carry or loading + grip

## What To Steal
- Basic patterns that support all strongman events.
- Moderate fatigue, frequent practice.
- Enough variety to build capacity without losing specificity.

## Blend Notes
- Transition into contest prep once the event list is known.
