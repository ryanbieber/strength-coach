# Strongman Axle and Loading

## Gist
- Axle press and loading block with a heavy awkward-object bias.
- Good when the contest rewards clean setup, bracing, and repeated loads.

## Best Use
- 4 day weeks.
- Athletes who need more axle work and loading practice.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP.
- Use it when the contest has axle and loading demands that need direct carryover.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: axle press
- Day 2: loading event
- Day 3: upper back and triceps
- Day 4: secondary load or carry

## What To Steal
- Axle-specific clean and rack work.
- Repeated load practice from contest-style distances.
- Support work that keeps the event slots productive.

## Blend Notes
- If the axle is missing, use a thick bar or fixed-implement press with the same rack challenge.
- Early weeks can keep a little more support volume, then later weeks should bias the exact axle and loading setup.
