# Strongman Stone Lap and Load

## Gist
- Stone-focused block that trains the lap, extension, and load.
- Good when the athlete needs to finish the lift instead of stalling at the lap.

## Best Use
- 3 to 4 day weeks.
- Stone-heavy contests or athletes who miss on the extension.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: stone loads
- Day 2: squat and posterior chain
- Day 3: stone singles or series
- Day 4: trunk and grip

## What To Steal
- Lap position practice.
- Repeated exposure to awkward extension.
- Short, focused support work.

## Blend Notes
- If stones are unavailable, use sandbags or odd objects that still force the lap.
