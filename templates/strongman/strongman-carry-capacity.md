# Strongman Carry Capacity

## Gist
- Carry-focused block for athletes who need more work capacity under load.
- Emphasis on repeated carries, posture, and recovering between efforts.

## Best Use
- 5 day weeks.
- Contests with multiple carry events or long event medleys.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: GPP.
- Use it to build the base work capacity that later SPP and peak blocks can express.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: heavy carry
- Day 2: squat or hinge support
- Day 3: carry variation
- Day 4: upper back and trunk
- Day 5: carry medley or conditioning

## What To Steal
- Multiple carry exposures each week.
- Support work that helps posture and breathing.
- A mix of heavy and moderate carries.

## Blend Notes
- Keep distances and loads close to the show when the contest is near.
- When the contest is still far away, keep the carry menu broader and use the block to build repeated effort capacity.
