# Strongman Medley Block

## Gist
- A block built around moving through contest events with minimal rest.
- Trains transitions, pacing, grip, and decision-making under fatigue.

## Best Use
- Medley-heavy contests.
- Athletes who lose time in transitions or grip under fatigue.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: medley runs
- Day 2: carry and load support
- Day 3: event order rehearsal

## What To Steal
- Contest order practice.
- Short rest windows.
- Fatigue-resistant support work.

## Blend Notes
- Keep accessory work low so medley quality stays high.
