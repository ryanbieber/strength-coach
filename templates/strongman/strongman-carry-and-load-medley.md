# Strongman Carry and Load Medley

## Gist
- Medley block built around alternating carries and loads.
- Good for athletes who need to stay moving after awkward pickups.

## Best Use
- 4 to 5 day weeks.
- Contests with mixed object events.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: carry medley
- Day 2: load event
- Day 3: squat or hinge support
- Day 4: carry-load repeat
- Day 5: recovery and grip

## What To Steal
- Task switching under fatigue.
- Event spacing that mimics the contest.
- Simple support work after the medley slot.

## Blend Notes
- Match the order of the implement sequence when possible.
