# Strongman Pull and Brace

## Gist
- Hinge and bracing block for deadlift, car pull, or frame work.
- Built for athletes who need more force off the floor and less torso collapse.

## Best Use
- 3 to 4 day weeks.
- Pull-dominant contests or athletes with poor bracing under load.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: GPP to early SPP.
- Good when the athlete needs more trunk and hinge base before heavy event exposure.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: main pull
- Day 2: trunk and upper back
- Day 3: pull variation or carry
- Day 4: optional light event technique

## What To Steal
- Heavy but controlled event pulls.
- Trunk work that supports the setup.
- Enough back work to keep posture honest.

## Blend Notes
- Choose the closest implement that matches the pull path and loading feel.
- Keep the block broader at first, then move toward event-specific pulls if the contest gets closer.
