# Strongman Frame Drag and Pull

## Gist
- Block for frame carries, frame deadlifts, and drag-style work.
- Good for athletes who need more lower-body drive and bracing under awkward loading.

## Best Use
- 4 day weeks.
- Contests with frame carries, frame deadlifts, or heavy awkward pulling.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: frame deadlift or pull
- Day 2: squat support and trunk
- Day 3: frame carry or drag
- Day 4: posterior chain and upper back

## What To Steal
- Keep the implement awkward.
- Use support work that carries over to bracing and start position.
- Practice the competition setup, not just the load.

## Blend Notes
- If the frame is missing, use trap bar, thick handles, or sled drags that keep the same feel.
