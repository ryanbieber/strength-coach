# Strongman Farmers Grip

## Gist
- Farmers walk block with a grip-first bias.
- Built to improve pick-up speed, walking posture, and hand endurance under load.

## Best Use
- 3 day weeks.
- Athletes who lose points on grip or pick-up speed.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: farmers + upper back
- Day 2: hinge + trunk
- Day 3: farmers variation + carry support

## What To Steal
- Long enough carry exposure to challenge the hands.
- Simple support work that leaves recovery for the main event.
- Frequent practice of the start and turn.

## Blend Notes
- Match handle thickness, walk length, and total duration as closely as possible.
