# Strongman

## Gist
- Event-specific template built around implements, carries, loading, medleys, and grip under fatigue.
- The goal is to train the contest demand, not just the movement pattern.

## Best Use
- Contest prep blocks.
- Athletes who need to rehearse exact events or close simulations.
- Training cycles where event order and fatigue management matter.

## What To Steal
- Main event work first, accessories second.
- Event order practice and pacing.
- Heavy specific work plus fatigue-resistant support work.
- Implement choice based on contest similarity, not just exercise name.

## Blend Notes
- If the exact implement is available, use it.
- If not, simulate loading position, range of motion, grip, duration, and fatigue as closely as possible.
- Keep the block narrow as the show approaches.
