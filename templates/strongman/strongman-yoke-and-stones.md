# Strongman Yoke and Stones

## Gist
- A contest block built around yoke carries and stone loads.
- Emphasis is on bracing, torso position, and moving well after the first hard effort.

## Best Use
- 4 to 5 day weeks.
- Athletes with yoke, stone, or carry-heavy contests.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: yoke + squat support
- Day 2: stone loads + posterior chain
- Day 3: upper back and trunk
- Day 4: yoke variation or carry medley
- Day 5: light recovery and grip

## What To Steal
- Repeat the contest entry, pick-up, and walk.
- Keep stone work close to the actual load path.
- Use short support work after the event slot.

## Blend Notes
- If stones are unavailable, use sandbags or awkward loads that match the lap and extension demand.
