# Strongman Deadlift and Yoke

## Gist
- Pull-focused block pairing deadlift variation work with yoke.
- Useful when the athlete needs both start strength and moving strength.

## Best Use
- 4 day weeks.
- Athletes who need more pull power and trunk stiffness.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: main deadlift event
- Day 2: yoke or carry
- Day 3: posterior chain and upper back
- Day 4: secondary pull or squat support

## What To Steal
- Specific pulling setup.
- Heavy but manageable yoke exposures.
- Support work that does not steal recovery from the pull.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the yoke close enough to the contest that posture stays honest.
