# Strongman Medley and Transition

## Gist
- Medley-focused block for contests that reward speed between events.
- Trains pacing, transitions, and staying organized under fatigue.

## Best Use
- 4 to 5 day weeks.
- Athletes who lose time between implements.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP to Peak.
- Best once the athlete is strong enough that transitions are the real limiter.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: medley run
- Day 2: carry or load support
- Day 3: press or pull event
- Day 4: medley rehearsal
- Day 5: light recovery or grip

## What To Steal
- Short rest between tasks.
- Event order rehearsal.
- Enough support work to keep the medley sharp.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the medley sequence stable long enough to learn it.
- The later the phase, the tighter the sequence should match the contest.
