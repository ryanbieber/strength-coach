# Strongman Deadlift Focus

## Gist
- Strongman block centered on deadlift variants, frame pulls, car deadlift, and bracing.
- Built to improve starting strength and lockout under event conditions.

## Best Use
- Pull-limited athletes.
- Contest prep when the deadlift event is a major limiter.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: main event pull
- Day 2: posterior chain and trunk
- Day 3: speed pull or secondary hinge

## What To Steal
- Event-specific pulling setup.
- Support work for bracing, hamstrings, and upper back.
- Enough specificity to match the event feel.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the implement or closest simulation as close as possible to the show.
