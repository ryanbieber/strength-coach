# Strongman Repetition Event

## Gist
- Block for repetition events like log reps, axle reps, or stone series.
- Focuses on repeated clean work, breathing, and staying efficient rep to rep.

## Best Use
- 3 day weeks.
- Contests with timed or rep-based events.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: repetition event practice
- Day 2: support pressing or pulling
- Day 3: repeat event under easier conditions

## What To Steal
- Repeated exposure to the event rhythm.
- Breathing and setup consistency.
- Small support dose that does not bury the repeat work.

## Blend Notes
- Keep rep targets and rest times close to the contest.
