# Strongman Press and Carry

## Gist
- Hybrid block for overhead work plus heavy carries.
- Built for athletes whose contest demands both standing presses and loaded walking.

## Best Use
- 3 to 4 day weeks.
- Contests with a press and a carry in the same block or close together.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP.
- Best once general base work is done and the block needs to link two contest qualities together.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: main press
- Day 2: carry
- Day 3: secondary press or front squat
- Day 4: optional press technique and trunk

## What To Steal
- Keep both event types in view.
- Use upper back and trunk work to support the rack and the walk.
- Keep the accessory list short.

## Blend Notes
- If the exact press implement is missing, keep the clean or setup movement as close as possible.
- The block should get less general and more exact as the contest gets closer.
