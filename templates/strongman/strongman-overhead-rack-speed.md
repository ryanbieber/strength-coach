# Strongman Overhead Rack Speed

## Gist
- Log and axle pressing block built to improve the clean, rack position, and first drive.
- The goal is faster overhead turnover without turning the block into a shoulder-only press cycle.

## Best Use
- 3 to 4 day weeks.
- Athletes whose overhead event starts in the rack and rewards quick position changes.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: log or axle press + upper back
- Day 2: carry or loading + triceps
- Day 3: secondary press variation + front squat
- Day 4: optional event technique or recovery work

## What To Steal
- Exact implement first.
- Clean and rack practice before heavy pressing.
- Small amounts of support work that do not bury the main event.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the rack and clean positions as close to the contest as possible.
- Reduce novelty once the event list is known.
