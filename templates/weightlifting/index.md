# Weightlifting

## Gist
- Technical template centered on snatch and clean and jerk performance.
- Built around positions, speed, receiving strength, and enough lower body strength to support the classic lifts.

## Best Use
- Skill-driven blocks.
- Pre-meet or pre-test peaking.
- Athletes who need more frequency on the classic lifts or their closest variations.

## What To Steal
- Frequent exposure to the snatch and clean and jerk.
- Pulls, front squats, and overhead receiving work.
- Positions and timing matter as much as load.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the competition lifts in the plan.
- Use variations only when they improve positions, speed, or receiving strength.
- Reduce noisy variations as the peak approaches.
