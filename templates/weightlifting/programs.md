# Weightlifting Program Catalog

This folder holds external weightlifting programs worth studying when the athlete needs snatch and clean & jerk specificity, classic lift frequency, or peaking structure.

These are reference points, not fixed scripts. Use them to understand position work, classic-lift frequency, and how good weightlifting plans peak.

## Core Picks

- Catalyst Athletics, `Weightlifting Beginner Level 1`
  - An early beginner program for athletes who have recently learned the snatch and clean & jerk.
  - Useful when you want the simplest official Catalyst progression into regular weightlifting training.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/104/Weightlifting-Beginner-Level-1/
- Catalyst Athletics, `Weightlifting Beginner Level 2`
  - An 8-week beginner program intended for athletes who already have a little weightlifting exposure.
  - Useful when you want a second beginner step with full warm-up, prep, mobility, and accessory work.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/105/Weightlifting-Beginner-Level-2/
- Catalyst Athletics, `Basic Strength Cycle`
  - A shorter cycle that emphasizes squatting, pulling, and weightlifting strength.
  - Useful when the athlete needs a strength-focused phase before a more classic-lift-heavy peak.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/27/Basic-Strength-Cycle/
- Catalyst Athletics, `3-Phase Cycle`
  - An 18-week program with distinct phases that end in a peak for snatch and clean & jerk attempts.
  - Useful when you want a full classic-lift cycle with a clear progression to testing.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/39/3-Phase-Cycle/

## Also Worth Studying

- Catalyst Athletics, `Strength by Feel Cycle`
  - A 9-week strength-emphasis cycle that relies more on feel than fixed percentages.
  - Useful when the athlete needs a flexible, autoregulated build phase.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/38/Strength-by-Feel-Cycle/
- Catalyst Athletics, `Classic / Position Cycle`
  - An 11-week cycle centered on position, posture, and pulling strength.
  - Useful when the athlete needs to rebuild positions and bar path quality.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-training-program/37/Classic-Position-Cycle/
