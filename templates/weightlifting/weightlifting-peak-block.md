# Weightlifting Peak Block

## Gist
- Shorter pre-meet block that shifts from volume to intensity while keeping the classic lifts in place.
- Built to reduce fatigue and sharpen technical expression.

## Best Use
- Final 4 to 8 weeks before a meet.
- Athletes who already have a good technical base.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: Peak.
- Use it after the athlete has already built enough technical and strength base.

## Weekly Shape
- Snatch emphasis day
- Clean and jerk emphasis day
- Squat and pull support day

## What To Steal
- High specificity.
- Lower accessory noise.
- Fewer variations as the peak approaches.

## Blend Notes
- The closer to the meet, the more the block should look like the competition itself.
- This is the sharpening phase, not the accumulation phase.
