# Russian Classic Cycle

## Gist
- Structured weightlifting cycle with planned volume and intensity waves.
- Balances classic lifts, pulls, and squats in a more traditional periodized format.

## Best Use
- Intermediate lifters.
- Blocks that need a predictable build toward a meet.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP.
- Use it when the athlete needs structured classic-lift preparation before the final peak.

## Weekly Shape
- Snatch and clean and jerk exposures
- Pulls and squats
- Variation lifts that target positions

## What To Steal
- Volume-to-intensity progression.
- Frequent competition lift practice.
- Squat and pull support.

## Blend Notes
- Good middle-ground template between pure skill work and hard peaking.
- This sits in the middle: more specific than base work, less sharp than the final pre-meet phase.
