# Chinese Block Cycle

## Gist
- Weightlifting block that emphasizes positions, squat strength, pulls, and repeated technical work.
- Often useful when the athlete needs a more systematic build rather than daily-max style work.

## Best Use
- Intermediate to advanced lifters.
- Blocks leading into a meet or test.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: SPP.
- Use it when the athlete needs a specific technical build with supportive squats and pulls.

## Weekly Shape
- Snatch and clean and jerk
- Pulls
- Front squat and overhead support

## What To Steal
- Position-first exercise selection.
- Supportive squat and pull work.
- More structure than random variation hopping.

## Blend Notes
- Keep the classic lifts central and let the variations solve the limiter.
- This is a specific build phase rather than a final taper.
