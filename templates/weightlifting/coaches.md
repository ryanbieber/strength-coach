# Weightlifting Coaches

This folder collects coaches and coaching systems worth studying for Olympic weightlifting programming.

## Core Picks

- Greg Everett / Catalyst Athletics
  - Owner and coach at Catalyst Athletics, with a long-running focus on Olympic weightlifting education and coaching.
  - Useful when you want a highly technical, position-first weightlifting perspective.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/articles/author/1/Greg-Everett/
- Catalyst Athletics coaching system
  - A complete weightlifting education and training system with many programs and coaching tools.
  - Useful when you want a systematic progression from beginner work to competitive peaking.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/
- Mike Burgener / Burgener Strength
  - Coach and educator whose methodology underpins the Burgener Strength system and course offerings.
  - Useful when you want a foundation-focused teaching system for the snatch and clean & jerk.
  - Source: https://burgenerstrength.com/about-level-1-new/
- USA Weightlifting coach education
  - The national governing body’s coaching education system for Olympic weightlifting.
  - Useful when you want a competition-focused coaching lens grounded in the sport’s official structure.
  - Source: https://www.usaweightlifting.org/coaching/coaching-courses

## Also Worth Studying

- Catalyst Athletics team coaches
  - A coaching environment built around competition lifts, positional strength, and individualized progression.
  - Useful when you want to compare how a mature weightlifting system handles different athlete stages.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/train/
- USAW-style competitive coaching
  - A competitive weightlifting lens that prioritizes technique, timing, and meet readiness.
  - Useful when you want to compare Catalyst's methods to broader weightlifting practice.
  - Source: https://www.catalystathletics.com/olympic-weightlifting-seminars/certification/
- Burgener Strength team
  - Coaches who teach the Burgener methodology through courses, programs, and athlete development.
  - Useful when you want a practical teaching system with clear movement progressions.
  - Source: https://burgenerstrength.com/the-team/
