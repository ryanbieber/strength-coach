# General Strength

## Gist
- Broad strength template for athletes who need general GPP, movement quality, and a flexible base.
- Useful when the goal is to build capacity before moving into a more specific block.

## Best Use
- Off-season training.
- Transition blocks between sport-specific phases.
- Athletes who need a simple, recoverable structure.

## What To Steal
- Balanced lower body, upper body, hinge, and core work.
- Moderate volume with submaximal loading.
- Enough flexibility to adapt to schedule and recovery.

## Blend Notes
- Good starting point when the goal is not yet a meet peak.
- Easy to narrow into powerlifting, strongman, weightlifting, or bodybuilding once the goal becomes specific.
