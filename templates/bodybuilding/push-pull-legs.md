# Push Pull Legs

## Gist
- Classic bodybuilding split organized by movement pattern and muscle group priority.
- Good for repeatable weekly volume and easy exercise slotting.

## Best Use
- Massing blocks.
- Athletes who like clear body-part structure.

## Weekly Shape
- Push: chest, delts, triceps
- Pull: back, rear delts, biceps
- Legs: quads, hamstrings, glutes, calves

## What To Steal
- Clear body-part split.
- Priority exercise first.
- Easy volume tracking.

## Blend Notes
- Adjust volume by body part recovery and lagging areas.
