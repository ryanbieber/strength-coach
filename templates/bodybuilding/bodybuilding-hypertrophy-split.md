# Bodybuilding Hypertrophy Split

## Gist
- A muscle-group-based hypertrophy template with priority work for lagging body parts.
- The goal is growth with manageable fatigue and repeatable exercise selection.

## Best Use
- Massing phases.
- Weak-body-part specialization.
- Prep phases where the athlete needs to keep priority muscles trained without excessive fatigue.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: push priority
- Day 2: pull priority
- Day 3: legs priority
- Day 4: upper body support or weak body part
- Day 5: arms, delts, or lagging muscle group work

## What To Steal
- Start the session with the priority muscle group.
- Keep exercise selection stable long enough to measure progress.
- Use volume landmarks to decide when to add or remove work.

## Blend Notes
- Use the least aggravating exercise that still loads the target muscle well.
- As the show approaches, keep the priority muscles trained while reducing unnecessary fatigue.
