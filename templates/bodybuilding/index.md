# Bodybuilding

## Gist
- Hypertrophy template built around muscle-group priority, volume landmarks, and fatigue management.
- Goal is to drive growth in the muscles that matter most while keeping joints and recovery in check.

## Best Use
- Massing phases.
- Weak-body-part emphasis.
- Prep blocks where the athlete needs targeted development without losing recovery.

## What To Steal
- Exercise order based on priority muscle groups.
- Repeatable weekly volume landmarks.
- Stable movements long enough to track progress.
- More work for lagging areas, less for already-dominant areas.

## Blend Notes
- Volume is usually the main dial.
- Use the least aggravating exercise that still loads the target muscle well.
- As the show approaches, reduce unnecessary fatigue and keep the priority muscles trained.
