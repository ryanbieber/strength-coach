# Arnold Split

## Gist
- High-volume bodybuilding split with chest/back pairing, shoulder emphasis, and leg days.
- A classic high-frequency approach for advanced hypertrophy work.

## Best Use
- Experienced lifters who recover well from volume.
- Massing blocks where physique detail matters.

## Weekly Shape
- Chest and back
- Shoulders and arms
- Legs
- Repeat with adjusted volume

## What To Steal
- Large exercise menu.
- High local fatigue paired with muscle-group focus.
- Repeatable weekly push on weak points.

## Blend Notes
- Best when the athlete can handle a lot of hard sets without joint issues.
