# Full-Body Hypertrophy

## Gist
- Full-body bodybuilding template that spreads volume across multiple weekly exposures.
- Good for frequency, fatigue management, and consistent technique practice.

## Best Use
- Athletes who grow better with higher frequency.
- Busy schedules where a few full-body sessions are easier to recover from.

## Weekly Shape
- Full body A
- Full body B
- Full body C

## What To Steal
- Spread hard sets across the week.
- Keep sessions efficient.
- Use exercise order to hit priority muscles early.

## Blend Notes
- Good default when you want body-part coverage without a long split.
