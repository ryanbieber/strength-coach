# Upper Lower Hypertrophy

## Gist
- Simple four-day hypertrophy split that balances training frequency and recovery.
- Good for athletes who want consistent upper and lower body exposure.

## Best Use
- General muscle gain.
- Intermediate bodybuilders who need a recoverable structure.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: GPP.
- Use it to build a broad hypertrophy base with manageable recovery cost.

## Weekly Shape
- Upper A
- Lower A
- Upper B
- Lower B

## What To Steal
- Repeatable upper/lower frequency.
- Easy progress tracking.
- Enough recovery to push hard sets.

## Blend Notes
- Useful when the athlete needs more frequency than a bro split but less complexity than a full-body rotation.
- This is general developmental work before narrower body-part emphasis.
