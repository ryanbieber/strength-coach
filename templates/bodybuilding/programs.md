# Bodybuilding Program Catalog

This folder holds external bodybuilding and hypertrophy programs worth studying when the athlete needs focused muscle growth or a higher-volume hypertrophy block.

These are reference points, not fixed scripts. Use them to understand volume landmarks, split structure, and fatigue management for hypertrophy.

## Core Picks

- Barbell Medicine, `Bodybuilding I – 2nd Generation`
  - A complete hypertrophy-focused system built around higher-volume, intelligently programmed training.
  - Useful when you want a flexible, evidence-based bodybuilding block with multiple split options.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/hypertrophy/bodybuilding-template/
- Barbell Medicine, `Bodybuilding II`
  - An advanced bodybuilding program with multiple hypertrophy blocks.
  - Useful when you want a more aggressive hypertrophy setup with clear block structure.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/hypertrophy/bodybuilding-ii-template-new/
- Barbell Medicine, `Bodybuilding Bundle`
  - A bundle containing both bodybuilding template families.
  - Useful when you want the broadest view of how Barbell Medicine structures hypertrophy work.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/hypertrophy/bodybuilding-template-bundle/
- Barbell Medicine, `Hypertrophy I – 2nd Generation`
  - A broader hypertrophy template that still tracks well with bodybuilding goals.
  - Useful when the athlete wants muscle growth with a slightly less specialized setup than Bodybuilding I or II.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/hypertrophy/hypertrophy-i-template/
