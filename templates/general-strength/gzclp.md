# GZCLP

## Gist
- General strength linear progression built around T1, T2, and T3 work.
- Flexible enough to build strength while keeping enough variation to train multiple qualities.

## Best Use
- Beginners and early intermediates.
- Athletes who want a structured but adaptable linear template.

## Weekly Shape
- T1: main lift for low reps and progressive loading
- T2: secondary compound lift for moderate reps
- T3: accessory and hypertrophy work

## What To Steal
- Tiered exercise selection.
- Main lift, secondary lift, accessory progression.
- Easy way to control volume and intensity.

## Blend Notes
- Good when the athlete needs structure with some flexibility.
