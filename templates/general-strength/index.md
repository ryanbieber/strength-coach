# General Strength Templates

## Gist
- A simple off-season strength and work-capacity template.
- Built to create a durable base before the athlete moves into a more specific block.

## Best Use
- General strength development.
- Transition blocks.
- Athletes who need a balanced template that is not tied to one sport yet.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: squat pattern + upper push + core
- Day 2: hinge pattern + upper pull + carry
- Day 3: full-body mixed work + conditioning or weak-point work

## What To Steal
- Keep the movements simple and repeatable.
- Use moderate volume with submaximal loading.
- Include some carry, core, or conditioning work to build tolerance.

## Blend Notes
- This folder holds simple base-building and novice-friendly programs.
- Use these when the goal is general preparedness rather than a sport-specific peak.
- These can be narrowed into powerlifting, strongman, weightlifting, or bodybuilding later.
