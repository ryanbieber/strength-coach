# Starting Strength Style

## Gist
- Beginner linear progression built around squat, press, deadlift, and a few simple accessory lifts.
- Built to teach technique and add strength quickly with minimal complexity.

## Best Use
- New lifters.
- General strength base blocks.
- Athletes who need a straightforward template with easy recovery management.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: squat, press, deadlift
- Day 2: squat, bench, power clean or row
- Day 3: squat, press, deadlift

## What To Steal
- Keep the exercise list short.
- Add load often while technique stays good.
- Use simple progression and conservative accessory volume.

## Blend Notes
- Good when the athlete is still learning the lifts.
- Trim volume first if recovery drops.
