# StrongLifts 5x5

## Gist
- Simple novice template built around squat, bench, press, and deadlift.
- Emphasizes repeatable 5x5 work and small load jumps.

## Best Use
- Beginners.
- Athletes who need a very easy-to-follow base program.
- Off-season general strength.

## Weekly Shape
- Day A: squat, bench, row
- Day B: squat, press, deadlift

## What To Steal
- Easy A/B rotation.
- Repeatable 5-rep work.
- Straightforward progression.

## Blend Notes
- Good for building consistency.
- Not for advanced peaking.
