# General Strength Program Catalog

This folder holds external general-strength programs worth studying when the athlete needs a simple base block, novice progression, or a time-efficient strength template.

These are reference points, not fixed scripts. Use them to understand progression, repeatability, and how general strength templates manage fatigue.

## Core Picks

- Barbell Medicine, `Beginner Program – 2nd Generation`
  - A 15-week beginner system with built-in progression, deloads, and conditioning.
  - Useful when the athlete is new to training or wants a simple ramp into barbell work.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/strength/beginner-template/
- Barbell Medicine, `General Strength & Conditioning I – 2nd Generation`
  - A complete strength and conditioning system designed to build muscle, improve endurance, and increase performance.
  - Useful when you want a balanced template that blends barbell work and conditioning.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/strength/general-strength-conditioning-template/
- Barbell Medicine, `Strength I – 2nd Generation`
  - A structured strength template centered on the squat, bench press, deadlift, and overhead press.
  - Useful when you want a clear jump from beginner work into a more formal strength block.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/strength/strength-i-template/
- Barbell Medicine, `Time Crunch – 2nd Generation`
  - A time-efficient strength and conditioning system built around limited training time.
  - Useful when the athlete needs good results inside a tight schedule.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/strength/time-crunch-template/
- Barbell Medicine, `Strength II – 2nd Generation`
  - A higher-volume strength template for lifters ready to progress beyond the most basic strength blocks.
  - Useful when the athlete needs more structure and workload after Strength I.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/shop/training-templates/strength/strength-ii-template/
