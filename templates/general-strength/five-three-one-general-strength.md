# 5/3/1 General Strength

## Gist
- Long-term conservative template built around a training max and repeatable cycles.
- Good for sustainable progress and general strength development.

## Best Use
- Off-season base building.
- Athletes who need steady progress without high fatigue.

## Weekly Shape
- Day 1: squat focus
- Day 2: press focus
- Day 3: deadlift focus
- Day 4: bench or upper body focus

## What To Steal
- Conservative training max.
- Simple top-set progression.
- Assistance work that matches the goal.

## Blend Notes
- Good when the athlete needs longevity and low decision fatigue.
