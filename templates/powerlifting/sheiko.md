# Sheiko

## Gist
- High-volume powerlifting template built around frequent submaximal practice.
- Lots of variation work and repeated exposure to the competition lifts.

## Best Use
- Technique practice.
- Work-capacity blocks.
- Athletes who tolerate a lot of volume and repeatable practice.

## What To Steal
- Frequent squat, bench, and deadlift exposure.
- Moderate loading with many quality sets.
- Variation choices that reinforce positions without overcooking the athlete.

## Blend Notes
- Good base-building template before a meet peak.
- Reduce volume first if recovery is limited.
