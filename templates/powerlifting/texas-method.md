# Texas Method

## Gist
- Three-day weekly structure with volume day, recovery day, and intensity day.
- Built for intermediate lifters who still respond to weekly progress.

## Best Use
- Simple weekly progression.
- Athletes who like a predictable weekly rhythm.

## What To Steal
- One higher-volume exposure.
- One lighter recovery day.
- One performance-focused day.

## Blend Notes
- Works well when recovery is decent and the athlete needs structure without too much complexity.
