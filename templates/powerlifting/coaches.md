# Powerlifting Coaches

This folder collects coaches and coaching systems worth studying for powerlifting programming.

## Core Picks

- Greg Nuckols / Stronger by Science
  - Founder of SBS and a major voice in evidence-based powerlifting programming.
  - Useful when you want flexible, data-informed strength plans.
  - Source: https://www.strongerbyscience.com/coaching/
- Mike Tuchscherer / Reactive Training Systems
  - RTS founder and a leading voice in individualized powerlifting programming and RPE-based coaching.
  - Useful when you want autoregulated programming that reacts to athlete readiness.
  - Source: https://store.reactivetrainingsystems.com/pages/mike-tuchscherer
- Bryce Lewis / The Strength Athlete
  - TSA founder and competitive elite powerlifter focused on custom training cycles, weekly coaching, and meet prep.
  - Useful when you want athlete-centric powerlifting coaching with explicit attempt selection and feedback loops.
  - Source: https://www.thestrengthathlete.com/bryce-lewis
- Eric Helms / 3D Muscle Journey
  - Coach and science communicator focused on sustainable pathways to high performance.
  - Useful when you want a powerlifting lens that also manages long-term fatigue.
  - Source: https://www.3dmusclejourney.com/about
- Chad Wesley Smith / Juggernaut Training Systems
  - Founder of JTS with a major role in modern strength coaching.
  - Useful when you want bridge blocks, off-season base building, and high-output training structure.
  - Source: https://www.jtsstrength.com/about/
- Westside Barbell
  - The official source of the Conjugate Method and a major influence on modern powerlifting.
  - Useful when you want a school of thought centered on max-effort, dynamic-effort, and specialized accessory work.
  - Source: https://www.westsidebarbell.com/

## Also Worth Studying

- Barbell Medicine coaching team
  - Evidence-based strength coaching with strong ties to practical programming and health-aware training.
  - Useful when the athlete wants powerlifting programming that is still flexible and sustainable.
  - Source: https://www.barbellmedicine.com/coaching/
- The Strength Athlete coaching team
  - Weekly coaching, custom training cycles, and direct competition prep with ongoing athlete feedback.
  - Useful when you want an organization built around responsive powerlifting coaching.
  - Source: https://www.thestrengthathlete.com/coaches
- Sheiko system
  - A classic powerlifting framework centered on structured repeated practice and competition specificity.
  - Useful when you want a well-known high-volume strength method to compare against modern autoregulation.
  - Source: https://sheikogold.com/
- Westside coaching pathway
  - A formal training and education pathway built around the Conjugate Method.
  - Useful when you want a structured system that explains the method instead of just the workouts.
  - Source: https://www.westsidebarbell.com/
