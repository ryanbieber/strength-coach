# Mag Ort

## Gist
- Deadlift-only peaking and overload template with a narrow focus.
- High fatigue, high specificity.

## Best Use
- Deadlift priority block.
- Athletes who can recover from a hard deadlift emphasis.

## Phase Bias
- Primary phase: Peak.
- Best after the athlete already has the base to tolerate a deadlift-only overload block.

## What To Steal
- Progressive deadlift-specific overload.
- Limited exercise selection.
- Clear taper runway.

## Blend Notes
- Use when the deadlift is the main event.
- Keep the block tight and purpose-built.
- This is very narrow, very specific, and should live late in the build.
